Checkpoint 1: 3 Retail Business Ideas

Jewelry Website

A company that sells hypoallergenic jewelry that doesn't tarnish or irritate skin. Perfect for anyone who has sensitive skin or is allergic to metals. The products will include necklaces, earrings (including clip-ons), and bracelets. Can include custom designs as well.

Skincare Website

A company dedicated to selling skincare for face and body that is sustainable, affordable, and customizable to fit people's skin type and concerns. These include cleanser, moisturizer, toner, sunscreen, serums, and etc.

Chinese Traditional Apparel

This company sells traditional clothing from Chinese culture. The clothing articles include Hanfu, Qipao (Chinese dress), Tang Suit (Chinese jacket), and Zhongshan suit.
